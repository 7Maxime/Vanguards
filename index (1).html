Mission, problem, and solution

These three statements define the purpose of our Think Tank, the challenge we identified in Bugesera, and the practical response we developed through research and field engagement.

Mission statement

“Our think tank mission is to bridge the gap between education and employment in Bugesera by transforming theoretical learning into practical agricultural skills.”

Problem statement

Bugesera district has a high number of unemployed youth between 16–35 years because of the mismatch between formal education and the job market. Many young people leave school with theoretical knowledge, but without enough practical agricultural, technical, and entrepreneurial skills to become job-ready or productive.

Solution statement

We propose a Youth Agribusiness Training and Incubation Program that combines hands-on agricultural training, entrepreneurship skills, mentorship, and market linkages so youth can view agriculture as a profitable enterprise rather than a last resort.

Why our solution is practical

Our research and field work showed that the problem is not just lack of interest. The real gap is lack of practice, business exposure, mentorship, and market readiness. That is why our prototype focuses on training that is experiential, applied, and connected to the realities of agribusiness.

Our prototype: Youth Agribusiness Training & Incubation Program

The prototype turns the solution into a practical implementation model. It translates our research into a pilot program that prepares young people to build agricultural careers and agribusiness ventures.

Pillar 1

Hands-on agricultural training

Participants gain practical exposure to crop management, irrigation basics, farm operations, and field-based learning.

Pillar 2

Entrepreneurship and incubation

Youth refine their agribusiness ideas through coaching, business planning, market thinking, and guided incubation.

Pillar 3

Mentorship and market linkage

Top teams receive mentor support, exposure visits, pitching opportunities, and linkages to real market actors.
